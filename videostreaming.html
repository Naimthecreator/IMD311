Anime

Anime is a kind of Japanese animation that is created or inspired by it.
It is the Japanese name for cartoon or animation, and it is used to
describe all cartoons, regardless of country. Outside of Japan, anime refers to
Japanese animation films defined by dazzling visuals, dynamic characters, and
appealing themes such as sci-fi, romance, and supernatural forces.

Jujutsu Kaisen


Yuuji Itadori, a high school student, spends his days either
participating in unfounded paranormal activities with the Occult Club or visiting his ill grandpa
in the hospital. However, when he unintentionally comes into contact
with a cursed artefact, his peaceful existence takes a bizarre turn. After ingesting the
alleged object, revealed to be a finger belonging to the
demon Sukuna Ryoumen, the King of Curses, Yuuji finds himself unexpectedly forced
into the domain of Curses—dreadful entities produced by human hate and negativity.




Eminence Of The Shadow


Everything has been going as planned, but the hour of
waking is approaching. Cid Kagenou and Shadow Garden examine
the Lawless City, a cesspool where three mighty kings rule
the streets and the red moon hangs low in
the sky. The ultimate pull for Cid, however, is one
who can draw blood-the Blood Queen, a vampire who has
slept for aeons in her coffin. Cid may face
a day of reckoning as her awakening approaches.




Fate: Strange Fake


A new Holy Grail War is about to begin in
the American metropolis of Snowfield. Such vicious battles pit magic
professionals known as magi against one other, who rise to
the rank of Master by calling Servants—heroic spirits from the
past. Any of the winner's requests are assumed to be
granted. However, it seems that the organisation responsible for the
current predicament has only managed to build a sloppy replica
of the Third Holy Grail War. As the strange
ceremony draws the attention of the Mage's Association in London,
Flatt Escardos, the young apprentice of Lord El-Melloi II—a past
participant in the Fourth Holy Grail War—travels to Snowfield and
joins the ranks of the Masters, much to his teacher's chagrin.
