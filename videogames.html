Video Games

A video game, often known as a computer game, is a kind of electronic game that may be manipulated
using an input device such as a gamepad, keyboard, or joystick. Video games may be used for pleasure and leisure,
as well as contests and computer learning. Some video games are intended to assist
people develop their fine motor skills and hand-eye coordination.

VALORANT

What is valorant?


It's a multiplayer first-person shooter (FPS) with 5v5 teams,
with one attacking and the other defending. Search and Destroy,
the basic game mode, is quite similar to CS:GO.The assaulting
side's purpose is to place a bomb (referred to as a spike) and
have it explode, while the defensive team attempts to prevent this.
Whether or whether the spike is placed, the opposing team will win
if a squad is wiped out before any other victory condition is satisfied.

GENSHIN IMPACT

What is Genshin Impact?


At first glance, Genshin Impact might look like just another anime
open-world game, but there are a lot of interesting things going on
that set it apart. Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG.
It was created and distributed by the Chinese studio miHoYo.
Players take control of a variety of party members, each with their
own set of talents, weapons, armour, and personalities. Real-time
combat allows players to use ranged, melee, and elemental attacks
against a diverse assortment of opponents in the game's
open world and dungeons.
